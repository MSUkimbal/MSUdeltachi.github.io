Hazing ∴

As stated in our second expectations, "I will respect the dignity and worth of all persons. I will not physically, mentally, psychologically or sexually abuse or haze any human being." Delta Chi strives to make the fraternity experience of an assosicate memeber one that is fun and self growing.





Cost ∴

It is true — the fraternity demands some sort of membership dues. Each new member is required to pay a one-time new member and initiation fee to the IHQ (International Headquaters). These dues are to cover the cost of events, lodging and other activities or expenses throughout the year. If you have more questions or concern please feel free to contact our treasurer.
