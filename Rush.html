Why You Should Rush

Delta Chi is a fraternity that sets itself apart from the others - our mission is to develop young men into adults that are prepared for the world in and after college. We set ourselves apart by following Delta Chi's core values:


To Promote Friendship, Develop Character, Advance Justice, and to Assist in the Acquisition of a Sound Education


Not only preparing our members for the professional world, Delta Chi emphasizes the importance of friendship and brotherhood - The members of Missouri State Delta Chi will be more than other fraternity members - they will be your brothers, a lasting fraternal bond will be made that will carry itself even after your own college years. Delta Chi is a life-long investment -- Your brothers now, in college, will be your brothers throughout your entire life.


Aside from brotherhood, Delta Chi stresses the importance of a Hazing free environment for all new members. We believe that all men, no matter their background, deserve a chance to be included in the fraternal experience. To achieve this, our new members will not be hazed, abused, or discriminated in any way - a fact that still sets us apart from many other fraternal organizations.


There's always plenty to do on campus as a Delta Chi. Delta Chi at Missouri State participates in intramural activities, competitions, and other FSL sponsored events on the campus throughout the year. Get involved the right way -- join an organization where you are surrounded by your best friends for life; Develops you into a man of character; Values justice over anything else, and will assist you academically...
